# Sample Discord Channel Structure (Gideon-Style)

This is a copyable default. Equivalent structure works in any messaging app.

## Suggested Channels

- `#ops-command` — owner requests, approvals, priorities
- `#guest-inquiries` — new inquiry intake, recommendation summaries
- `#booking-desk` — approvals/rejections, booking updates, notes
- `#guest-comms` — outgoing/incoming communication summaries
- `#pricing-harness` — market pricing runs and recommendation outputs
- `#ops-log` — cron status, maintenance, routine health checks
- `#handoff` — staff-facing notes and day-of reminders

## Rules of Use

- Keep approvals centralized in `#ops-command` or `#booking-desk`.
- Keep raw diagnostics out of guest-facing channels.
- Use one source of truth for each decision thread.
