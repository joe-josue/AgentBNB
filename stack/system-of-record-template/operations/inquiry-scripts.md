# Inquiry Scripts

## Holding Reply (Default)
"Hi {{first_name}}, thanks for your inquiry. We received your request and are checking availability now. We will confirm shortly."

## Clarification Request
"Thanks, {{first_name}}. To recommend the best setup, can you confirm {{missing_field}}?"

## Approval Follow-up
"Great news, your booking is approved. Here are your confirmed stay details: {{booking_summary}}"

## Decline Message
"Thank you for your interest. We are unavailable for your requested dates. If you want, we can help suggest alternative dates."

## Notes
Keep tone consistent with brand voice and keep these scripts synced with website + OTA messaging.
